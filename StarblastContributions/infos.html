Starblast Contributions informations
Welcome to this page where are registered informations about this website.
What is this website?
This website shows up all people who contributed in any way to the game Starblast.io. People who are present in the contributors list are people who proved their capacity of promoting, helping, improving the game and community, thanks to platforms such as Discord, Reddit, Youtube, Twitch; or the provided tools given by the developers themselves: the ship editor, the mod editor, or the game itself.
Some people present here are showed up because of their capacity of playing the game, which means because they participated and won recognized events such as SDC or Racing Championship (ended).
How can I appear here?
As stated above, someone appears here once they achieved something in the community.
People usually needs to spend a lot of times and efforts - at their scale - into their actions to be able to really contribute to the game itself.
warning Notice that some people / some roles in this list are not something 100% recognized. Roles such as Tool Creator, Codes Creator, are not roles applied in the Discord server. Roles such as Youtube does not lead to the contributor role too. But, in any way, those people contributed and put time and efforts to do something for this community, and that is the reason why they are present in here; their works are recognized.
Is everyone here?
No, not every contributors of the starblast community are present in here.
Some people may have been missed, and the administrators of this website are ready to put them in the contributor list as long as they contributed to the game. If you are one of these person or if you know one of these person, feel free to join the Support discord server to report it.
Also, some people who contributed to the game does not wish to appear. For that reason, they were removed or "blacklisted" from this list.
Are there really requirements?
The only requirement to appear in here is to contribute to the game, as stated above.
warning Some people cannot appear in the list, because of some reasons:
- They were / are banned from the game
- They shared an ECP key
- They used hacks and such to ruin people's gameplay
Is this website safe?
It completely is. This website does run scripts only for list show-up and such. No cookies nor datas are taken from your device.
The code of this project is open-source on Github.
More informations?
This website isn't supported by the Official Starblast Discord Server nor by the developers of the game. It is at first a project made for fun, something made because of boredom.
This website may still have some developement issues, or some graphics improvement. For any possible ideas, suggestions, or report, do not hesitate to join the Support server. To meet the contributors, join the Official Starblast Discord server.
